# Cinematic Camera for GMod

This is my first ever GMod addon so go easy on me lol. It's a free cam you can use to fly around and film cool shots without messing up your actual movement or aim. I made this because I wanted to film some machinima style stuff and got tired of the camera messing with my WASD and shooting.

## What it can do

You can fly the camera around with the arrow keys and hold Alt to look around instead of move. There's also a bunch of different camera modes you can switch between like Top-Down, Shoulder, Chase, Crane, Orbit, and even a Dolly Zoom one that does that trippy vertigo zoom thing from movies. There's a Drone mode too, a low angle Hero cam, and a Side-Scroller mode if you want it to look like a 2D game.

There's also a Bone Cam which lets you stick the camera onto your actual player model, like your head, chest, hands, or feet, and it moves with your character in real time. Heads up though, the body cams are still a bit buggy, some of them clip into the body a little depending on your player model and the movement can feel weird sometimes especially with custom models. Still working on making it better.

You can also turn on black bars and hide your HUD to make it feel more like an actual movie, and there's a shake option and a tilt option if you want a more shaky handheld camera vibe.

Oh and if you go AFK for a bit it randomly starts filming stuff near you like other players or vehicles, kinda like how GTA does it when you leave your character alone.

Everything's controlled from a menu, just press C and you'll see the camera icon.

## How to install it

Just download this whole repo and drop the folder into your garrysmod/addons folder, then restart the game. Press C and look for the camera icon in the menu.

## Controls

Arrow keys move the camera when you're in Free Fly mode. Hold Alt and press arrows to look around instead. Page Up and Page Down move it up and down.

Everything else you can just click around in the menu, no need to remember commands.

## Bugs / stuff that still needs work

- Bone cam still clips into the body sometimes depending on the part and the model
- Some body cams can feel a little laggy or shaky when you're moving fast
- Might not work perfectly with every custom player model since bone names can be different

This is still a work in progress since it's my first addon, so if something breaks feel free to poke around the code, most of it is labeled.
